Tasty Lasagna

Lasagna is a rich, layered Italian dish made with sheets of pasta, hearty meat sauce, creamy ricotta, and melted mozzarella. It's a comfort food favorite that's perfect for feeding a crowd or enjoying as leftovers. Baked until bubbly and golden, lasagna offers a delicious blend of flavors and textures in every bite, making it a go-to choice for family gatherings or cozy dinners.

Ingredients

  • 9 lasagna noodles
  • 1 lb ground beef or Italian sausage
  • 3 cups marinara sauce
  • 15 oz ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 tbsp chopped fresh parsley (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook lasagna noodles according to package instructions; drain and set aside.
  3. In a skillet, cook ground beef or sausage over medium heat until browned; drain excess fat.
  4. Stir marinara sauce into the cooked meat and simmer for 5 minutes.
  5. In a bowl, mix ricotta cheese with egg, parsley, salt, and pepper.
  6. Spread a thin layer of meat sauce on the bottom of a baking dish.
  7. Layer 3 noodles over the sauce, then spread 1/3 of the ricotta mixture, 1/3 of the meat sauce, and sprinkle with mozzarella.
  8. Repeat layers two more times, ending with remaining sauce and mozzarella on top.
  9. Sprinkle Parmesan cheese over the top.
  10. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes.
  11. Remove foil and bake for an additional 20 to 25 minutes until bubbly and golden.
  12. Let rest for 10 to 15 minutes before slicing and serving.
